Customer Sale Car Service Dealer Invoice

    Key Features:

  • Sales Operations: Salespeople sell cars to customers, and each car is associated with a single salesperson and customer. Invoices are generated per transaction, ensuring accurate record-keeping.
  • Customer Service: Customers can have their cars serviced at the dealership, even without purchasing a car. Service tickets are generated for each car brought in, and detailed service history is maintained.
  • Mechanic Assignment: Multiple mechanics can work on a car, and each mechanic can handle multiple cars. The system tracks service work and the parts used during the repair.

    Technologies Used:

  • Backend: PHP handles the core logic of the system, processing requests such as sales, customer management, and service tracking.
  • Database: MySQL stores all data related to sales, customers, cars, service tickets, and more, ensuring that the system runs smoothly with efficient data management.
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript form the user interface, making the system interactive and easy to use.
